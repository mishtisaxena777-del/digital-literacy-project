#  Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## -> Objective
To create a one-page infographic that explains the concept of digital literacy and its importance.

## ->Tool Used
Canva (or mention your tool)

## -> Topics Covered
- What is Digital Literacy  
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Useful Digital Tools for Students  
- Professional Online Presence  
- Email Etiquette  

## -> Contents
- Infographic image/screenshot

## -> Description
This infographic visually represents key aspects of digital literacy in a simple and engaging format. It helps students understand how to use digital tools effectively and stay safe online.

## -> Learning Outcome
Learned how to present information visually and understood the importance of digital awareness.
