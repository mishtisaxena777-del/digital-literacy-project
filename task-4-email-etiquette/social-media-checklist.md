#  Social Media Use – Checklist

✔️ Do’s
1. Think before you post – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2. Maintain privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information.
3. Be respectful – Engage politely in discussions and respect different opinions.
4. Verify information – Check facts before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
5. Use social media productively – Follow educational pages, join study groups, and build your network.
___________________________________________ 
❌ Don’ts
1. Don’t overshare personal details – Avoid posting sensitive information like address, phone number, etc.
2. Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It can harm your reputation.
3. Don’t post inappropriate content – Offensive or unprofessional posts can affect your future opportunities.
4. Don’t believe everything you see online – Be cautious of fake news and scams.
5. Don’t let social media affect your studies – Avoid excessive usage that distracts from academics.
