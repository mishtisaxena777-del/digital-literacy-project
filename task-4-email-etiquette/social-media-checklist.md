Do and don’t
Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist for College Students
✔️ Do’s
•	Think before you post – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. 
•	Maintain privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information. 
•	Be respectful – Engage politely in discussions and respect different opinions. 
•	Verify information – Check facts before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation. 
•	Use social media productively – Follow educational pages, join study groups, and build your network. 
❌ Don’ts
•	Don’t overshare personal details – Avoid posting sensitive information like address, phone number, etc. 
•	Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It can harm your reputation. 
•	Don’t post inappropriate content – Offensive or unprofessional posts can affect your future opportunities. 
•	Don’t believe everything you see online – Be cautious of fake news and scams. 
•	Don’t let social media affect your studies – Avoid excessive usage that distracts from academics.
