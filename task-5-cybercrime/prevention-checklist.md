 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
 General Safety Tips
✔ Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
✔ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible
✔ Do not click on unknown or suspicious links in emails/SMS
✔ Avoid sharing personal information on untrusted websites
✔ Always log out from public/shared computers
✔ Install apps only from official app stores (Play Store/App Store)
________________________________________
 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
✔ Always verify the receiver’s name before making any UPI payment
✔ Never approve unknown “Collect Requests” on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe
✔ Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank
✔ Avoid making payments using public Wi-Fi networks
 Awareness & Action
✔ Be cautious of offers that seem “too good to be true”
✔ Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
✔ Update your phone and apps to the latest security versions
