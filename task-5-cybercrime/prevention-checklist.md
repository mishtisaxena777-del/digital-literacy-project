 # Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
 
 General Safety Tips
1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links in emails/SMS
4. Avoid sharing personal information on untrusted websites
5. Always log out from public/shared computers
6. Install apps only from official app stores (Play Store/App Store)
________________________________________
 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
1. Always verify the receiver’s name before making any UPI payment
2. Never approve unknown “Collect Requests” on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe
3. Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank
4. Avoid making payments using public Wi-Fi networks
________________________________________
 Awareness & Action
1. Be cautious of offers that seem “too good to be true”
2. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
3. Update your phone and apps to the latest security versions
________________________________________
Report Cybercrime Immediately
1. Report online fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24×7)
